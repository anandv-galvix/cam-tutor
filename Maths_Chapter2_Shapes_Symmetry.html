🧩 Can Triangles Tessellate?

Tessellation means shapes fit together with no gaps and no overlaps! 🧱

✅ All triangles can tessellate!
Fun Fact! 🌟
Every type of triangle — equilateral, isosceles AND scalene — can tessellate. You can always fit triangles together without gaps!

🧠 Think Like a Mathematician!

Challenge: Can a triangle have TWO right angles? 🤔

Remember: all three angles in a triangle add up to 180°. One right angle = 90°. Two right angles = 180°... but there'd be nothing left for the third angle! So the answer is NO — it's impossible! 🎉

Scalene ✓ can have a right angle Isosceles ✓ can have a right angle Equilateral ✗ cannot have a right angle

🔺 Which Triangles Have Which Angles?

🟢 Equilateral Triangle: All 3 angles are 60° — all ACUTE! Can never have a right or obtuse angle.
🟠 Isosceles Triangle: Can be all-acute, or have ONE obtuse angle, or ONE right angle (then it's a right-isosceles triangle!).
🟡 Scalene Triangle: Can have acute, obtuse, OR right angles. The most flexible triangle!

🪞 What is Symmetry?

A line of symmetry divides a shape into two halves that are mirror images of each other! 🦋

If you fold along the line, both halves match perfectly.

🧠 Think Like a Mathematician!

On a 3×3 grid, how many ways can you shade 4 squares to make a pattern with at least 1 line of symmetry?

Try it! Remember — patterns that look the same when you rotate them count as ONE pattern. 🔄

Think about the vertical, horizontal, and diagonal mirror lines through the centre of the grid. A pattern is symmetric if both halves match when folded along one of these lines!

🔍 Mirror Reflections

When a pattern is reflected, each point moves to the other side of the mirror line — the same distance away! 🏃

Reflection in a vertical mirror line
Remember the rules! 📏
• Each point reflects to the same distance on the other side
• The shape stays the same size
• Only the position changes — it flips!
