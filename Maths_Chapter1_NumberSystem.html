🔵 Rounding to the Nearest Whole Number

Rounding makes numbers easier to work with! 🐘

Real Life Example: It's easier to say "an African elephant is about 3 metres tall" than "the average elephant is 3.3 metres tall"!

📏 The Rounding Rule

↓
If the tenths digit is 0, 1, 2, 3 or 4 → Round DOWN (keep the whole number)
↑
If the tenths digit is 5, 6, 7, 8 or 9 → Round UP (add 1 to the whole number)

✏️ Worked Solutions

Step-by-step answers for each question.

Question 1 — Value of Digit 3 (3 marks)

a) 606 456.35 → The 3 is in the tenths column → value = 0.3
b) 3 030.03 → The 3 is in the millions… wait — 3 030.03 is a 4-digit number → The 3 is in the thousands column → value = 3 000
c) 100 300.7 → The 3 is in the hundreds column → value = 300
Answers: a) 0.3  |  b) 3 000  |  c) 300

Question 2 — Write in Digits (4 marks)

a) 100 000 + 3 000 + 500 + 7 + 0.9
= 100 000 + 3 000 + 500 + 7 + 0.9 = 103 507.9
b) 600 000 + 60 000 + 600 + 6 + 0.06
= 660 606.06
Answers: a) 103 507.9  |  b) 660 606.06

Question 3 — Missing Numbers (3 marks)

a) 358 × 100 → digits shift 2 places LEFT → 35 800
b) 2 700 ÷ ? = 27 → 2 700 ÷ 27 = 100, so the missing number is 100
c) ? × 1 000 = 8 000 → 8 000 ÷ 1 000 = 8
Answers: a) 35 800  |  b) 100  |  c) 8

Question 4 — Rounding (4 marks)

a) 65.8 → tenths digit = 8 (≥5) → Round UP → 66
b) 101.1 → tenths digit = 1 (<5) → Round DOWN → 101
c) 44.4 → tenths digit = 4 (<5) → Round DOWN → 44
d) 55.5 → tenths digit = 5 (≥5) → Round UP → 56
Answers: a) 66  |  b) 101  |  c) 44  |  d) 56

Question 5 — Sofia's Number (3 marks)

Sofia multiplied by 10 three times, so she multiplied by 10 × 10 × 10 = 1 000
To find the starting number, divide by 1 000:
20 000 ÷ 1 000 = 20
Check: 20 × 10 = 200 → × 10 = 2 000 → × 10 = 20 000 ✓
Answer: Sofia started with 20

Question 6 — Smallest and Largest (3 marks)

We need a 1 d.p. number that rounds to 231.
Rounds UP to 231: tenths digit must be 5, 6, 7, 8 or 9 → but whole part must be 230 → e.g. 230.5, 230.6 … 230.9
Rounds DOWN to 231: tenths digit must be 0, 1, 2, 3 or 4 → whole part is 231 → e.g. 231.0, 231.1 … 231.4
Smallest possible: 230.5 (tenths = 5 → rounds UP to 231)
Largest possible: 231.4 (tenths = 4 → rounds DOWN to 231)
Smallest: 230.5  |  Largest: 231.4
